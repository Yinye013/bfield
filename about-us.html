Introduction.

Barleyfield Limited is a Civil/Structural Engineering Contractors registered under the Company and Allied Matters Decree of 1990 with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria

BARLEYFIELD NIG. LTD. (RC. 447226)

We undertake Civil and Structural Engineering works, Building and Building Engineering services, Sub-soil Investigation, Piling, Telecommunication Infrastructure services and Electrical Engineering jobs for various organization and clients.
